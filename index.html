Is a trip to Michigan on your list?

Have you ever wanted to tour one of Michigan's most iconic vineyards and wineries? If so let us help you plan a Traverse City Wine Tour on the Old Mission Peninsula. We will take you on a five hour tour where you get to stop at and explore four wineries. At each winerey you will be able to tour the grounds, be shown around where the wine is made, and also get a chance to taste wine that was bottled fresh that same exact day. So what are you waiting for? Tour Traverse City and it's wineries today!

Michigan's State Parks are another hidden gem that exist all throughout Michigan. Parks are accessible throughout the day for free with a Parks Pass or you can get daily tickets at any entrance. These parks range from boardwalks and sand dunes, to nature trails and campsites. Michigan's State Parks the perfect opportunity for a relaxing weekend with your significant other or even you family! Find out today which parks are closest to you so you can start your adventure now!

Beaches, Campsites, and Lighthouses

Explore the Great Lakes of Michigan today by booking a date at one of our beautiful beaches across the state. We offer campsites at each location for families traveling in RV's. There is also daily parking from 10am-6pm every single day.

What to Wear

If it is a hot day out, make sure to bring the shade and some sunscreen! Temperatures can get very hot and exposure to sun is to be expected. Don't forget your sandles and a swimsuit because you will not want to miss out on our beautiful lakes. Once the sun goes down, make sure to have something to bundle up with because temperatures can drop significantly. Be prepared to best enjoy your beach experience along the shores of Michigan.

Reservations

We accept reservations two weeks ahead of a date, or further. If you are interested, please contact the Department of Natural Resources to guarantee a spot today! Reservations are first come first serve so make sure you don't miss out!
